About E-Reporter

E-Reporter is a platform that helps speed up the election process by enabling any member of the public to participate in the election reporting process. If you are a member of the public at a certain pollingstation where result counting is complete and the pink sheet has been posted, take a picture of the it and post it to this platform. This result is approved by an administrator if all condition are met. This platform also enables members to view the current official results of candidates in regions.
